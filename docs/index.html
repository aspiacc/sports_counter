Sports Counter - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Sports Counter does not actively collect, store, or transmit personal data. The app integrates the Google AdMob SDK to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, usage data, and approximate location to deliver personalized or non-personalized ads according to the Google Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Information

  • Data handled by AdMob is processed solely for ad delivery, reporting, and fraud prevention. Sports Counter does not access or store this information.
  • Locally, the app stores team names, scores, selected theme, and language preferences using SharedPreferences. This information never leaves your device.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share user data with any third parties beyond the AdMob integrations described above.

4. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13 years of age. If you are under 13, please use the app only with parental consent.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

6. Contact
